  <h2>About</h2>
  <p>
    Readylytics reads Health Connect data such as sleep, heart rate, HRV,
    exercise, and steps, then calculates local wellness scores for sleep,
    readiness, and activity. The app is designed for personal trend tracking,
    not diagnosis or treatment.
  </p>
  <p>
    Your health data stays on your device. Readylytics uses local storage for
    app data and local encrypted backup files for backup and restore.
